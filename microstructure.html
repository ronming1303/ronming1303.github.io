About This Data

This dashboard displays order book imbalance data collected from Coinbase's BTC/USD trading pair. The imbalance metric measures the relative volume difference between bid and ask sides at the top 10 levels.

Imbalance Formula:
I_t = (Σ V_bid(t) - Σ V_ask(t)) / (Σ V_bid(t) + Σ V_ask(t))

Data Collection:
Minute-level samples are collected automatically every hour. The hourly summary reports mean, median, min, and max values.
